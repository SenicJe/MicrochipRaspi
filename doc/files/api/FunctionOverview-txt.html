Devices

Depending on the configuration the 3DTouchPad identifies itself as one of two different devices with different functionality.  The devices as used in the table below are:

3DTP3DTouchPad (VID=04D8, PID=09D3)
3DTP-BL3DTouchPad in bootloader mode (VID=04D8, PID=09D4, see 2D Firmware Update)
