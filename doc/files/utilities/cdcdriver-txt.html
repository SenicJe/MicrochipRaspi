CDC Driver

The CDC-Driver is required for communication with Hillstar or Sabrewing-devices via USB-Bridge.  The Windows Installer of the 3DTouchPad SDK automatically installs this driver during setup.  It could be also found in utilities\usb_drivers\cdc\Windows.
